Vegan Grilled Cheese with Jalapeno & Avocado

Ooey and gooey vegan grilled cheese with pickled jalapenos and avocado

This is sure to please any grilled cheese lover. Tangy and spicy jalapenos, rich avocado and melted cheese on buttered sourdough bread. No one will ever be able to tell its vegan!

Ingredients:
  • 2 slices sourdough bread
  • 2 slices vegan cheese
  • 1/2 avocado
  • 1/4 cup pickled jalapenos
  • vegan butter for spreading on bread
Steps:
  1. Heat grill or pan on med heat
  2. Butter both slices of bread and place on heated pan
  3. Put one slice of cheese on top of bread
  4. When cheese is beginning to melt add avocado and jalapenos to one slice of bread, top with other slice
  5. Cook until bread is desired level of brown
  6. Remove from pan and enjoy!
