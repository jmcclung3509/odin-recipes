Chocolate Chip Cookies

Delicious and easy chocolate chip cookies you won't believe are vegan

These cookies are perfectly sweet, moist and chewy. No one will guess they are actually vegan!

Ingredients
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1.5 tsp baking powder
  • 3/4 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup oil
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1 tbsp vanilla
  • 1 cup vegan chocolate chips
Steps:
  1. Preheat oven to 350F. Line cookie sheet with parchment
  2. Add to a large bowl: flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt and mix
  3. To another bowl add oil, water, vanilla, sugars and mix very well until sugar incorporated
  4. Add dry to wet and gently mix until no dry flour remains
  5. Add to 2tbsp balls to baking sheet, leaving plenty of space between. Bake 9-11 mins, checking at 9 mins until desired brownness achieved.
  6. Cool slightly and enjoy
