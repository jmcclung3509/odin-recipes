Vegan Lasagna

Delicious vegan lasagna your family will love!

Vegan lasagna layered with cashew ricotta cheese, red lentil bolognese and loads of fresh spinach. This lasagna is packed with flavor, protein, fiber and is sure to satisfy!

Ingredients:
  • 1 cup dried red lentils
  • 2 jars favorite store-bought marinara
  • 2 cups cashew ricotta cheese
  • 4 cups baby spinach
  • 1 box lasagna noodles
  • Handful fresh basil
Steps:
  1. Preheat oven to 375F
  2. Cook lentils in marinara sauce on the stove until soft
  3. Add 1/2 cup tomato sauce to bottom of baking dish, cover with single layer of noodles.
  4. Top noodle with 1 cup cheese and 1/2 cup sauce
  5. Repeat until ingredients are gone
  6. Cover and bake until sauce is bubbly and cheese is brown(~30 mins)
  7. Uncover and cook another 1-15 mins until cheese is desired brown
  8. Enjoy!
